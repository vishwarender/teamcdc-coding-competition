About the Competition

This competition is designed to challenge students from grades 6 to 10 to develop creative and functional software projects. Participants can use various programming languages depending on their grade level, from Scratch for beginners to more advanced languages like Python, JavaScript, and Java for senior students.

Projects will be judged based on creativity, functionality, UI design, code cleanliness, and presentation. Winners will receive awards, and all participants will receive certificates of participation.
